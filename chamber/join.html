Membership Benefits

NP Membership Free

Designed for non-profit organizations. This membership offers access to community events and visibility in our directory. A great way to stay connected without any cost.

Bronze Membership

Ideal for small businesses. Includes all NP benefits plus a business profile listing on our website, and invitations to networking events and chamber meetings.

Silver Membership

Perfect for growing organizations. Includes all Bronze benefits, access to professional development workshops, spotlight positions on our homepage, and early registration for events.

Gold Membership

Premium tier for maximum exposure. Includes all Silver benefits plus featured advertising in our newsletter, discounted booth space at events, priority listings, and sponsorship opportunities.
